Celebrating 15 years of Excellence

Our Legacy

Alfaran Carriage Company, with 15 years of dedicated service, has transformed Punjab's transportation landscape. From a visionary inception to overcoming early challenges, we've grown into a fleet of 200+ vehicles, enhancing accessibility and mobility. Our collaborative network thrives through relationships with drivers, customers, and communities, ensuring top-notch service quality and operational efficiency. With a customer-centric approach, we've embraced technological advancements like mobile apps and GPS tracking, offering seamless travel experiences. Our commitment extends to sustainability, reflected in eco-friendly practices and community engagement. We've contributed not only to transportation but also to employment opportunities, fostering careers in the sector. Recognized for our impact, we're proud of our legacy. Looking ahead, our focus remains on growth, service excellence, and shaping Punjab's transportation future.
